FAQ
Common questions
Is it really free for 30 days? +
Yes, 30 days free with full access. You pay ₹1 upfront to set up the auto-pay mandate. After 30 days, ₹199/month or ₹1699/year is auto-charged.
Why do I pay ₹1? +
The ₹1 is a Razorpay mandate registration fee to set up your UPI/card for autopay. This is standard for Indian subscriptions. No other charges for 30 days.
When does my billing cycle start? +
Your 30-day free trial starts from the day you sign up. Your first ₹199 charge happens exactly on day 30 from your signup date. Every month after that on the same date.
Can I pause or cancel anytime? +
Yes. Pause anytime — no charges while paused. Cancel anytime with no questions asked. You keep access until end of your paid period.
What payment methods are accepted? +
UPI Autopay (Google Pay, PhonePe, Paytm), Debit cards, Credit cards, Net banking — all via Razorpay.
Does it work on mobile? +
Yes — works well on mobile browsers. A dedicated iOS and Android app is on the roadmap.
